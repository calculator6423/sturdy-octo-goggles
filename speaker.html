Strike a Pose!

Our daily yoga calendar. Every day is yoga day!

Saturday, June 29

The Classic: Butterfly

Sit down on your bottom and join the soles of your feet together with your hands. Lower your knees and then push them back up again, flapping like a butterfly's wings. You're doing it! Don't forget to keep your back straight!

Sunday, January 30

Child's Pose

Fun Fact: Child's pose can also be called Balasana! In this pose, you want to go onto your hands and knees and keep the tops of your feet on the floor with your knees bent on your yoga mat. Stretch your up torwads the sky and bring them down torwards the floor with the stomach between your knees and your palms flat on the floor. Relax your shoulders. Do you feel that nice stretch in your back and ankles? Don't bend your head, and remember to take deep, steady breaths.

Monday, January 31

Cat and Cow Pose: Are you a cat person? If so, you'll love this pose, and your health will too!

Kneel on your hands and knees. Your hands should be just under your shoulders (common mistake!) and your knees below your hips. Take a deep inhale and look up torwads the ceiling, stretching your stomach, and then exhale by rounding your back torwards the ceiling and looking down at your stomach. Repeat this as many times as you feel necessary.

Tuesday, February 1

Happy first day of a new month! Today we'll be doing downward dog. Woof!

Stand straight on your yoga mat, and then slowly let your hands fall onto the floor. Walk your self to where you can feel a stretch in your core, or where your body should form a triangle. Keep both toes pointing straight in front of you and breathe. If you need to, stretch out your arms a little bit. For a modified position, bend your knees just a little bit, but make sure you can still feel that stretch in your back and shoulders. Remember to take breaks!

Wednesday, February 2

Cobra Pose. You can do it- I believe in you!

Lie flat on your stomach and place your palms on the mat. Tilt your head back untill your chin points forward. Make sure you can feel that stretch! Roll your shoulders back and forth to relieve tension. Keep your head and chest up!

Thursday, February 3

Let's work on Posture: Mountain Pose

Stand on the mat. Lift yoru chest, broaden your back. Keep your core engaged and shoulders relaxed. Inhale. Feel that tension subside. Exhale. Your chin should be parallel to the floor. How do you feel?

If you want to, raise your hands uptorwads the ceiling and rise to your tippy toes. Inhale and exhale as needed, making sure to let your body relax.
